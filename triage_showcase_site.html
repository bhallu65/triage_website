Patient Safety
Zero Under-Triage Architecture
Red Flag Engine

50+ hard-coded clinical rules. Cannot be overruled by ML model.

risk_final = max(risk_ml, risk_redflag)
Without it: 8.3% under-triage
Confidence Escalation

Below τ=0.55 confidence → bump prediction up one tier.

risk_adj = risk_pred + 1, if max(P) < 0.55
Without it: 1.2% under-triage
Disagreement Detection

When branches diverge by ≥2 levels → take worse prediction.

risk = max(r_vitals, r_nlp), if |Δ| ≥ 2
Without it: 0.4% under-triage
Combined Result

All three layers → 0% under-triage on test set (n=1000). Over-triage: 6.8%.

Critical Sensitivity: 97.4% | Emergency Sensitivity: 95.3%
